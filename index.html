📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎮 All In is an interactive visual novel where your choices shape the story.

  • 📜 Read the dialogue – Each scene presents a story snippet
  • 🤔 Make choices – You'll have 4 options per scene
  • ✨ Good/Bad indicators – Colored badges show the moral alignment
  • 🔄 Restart anytime – Click RESTART to begin a new journey
  • 🎯 Multiple endings – Your decisions lead to 10+ unique endings

Every choice matters. Every decision changes your destiny.
